Tour Bandon

West Coast Game Park
Drive a short way out of town to access this safari-style wildlife park. You can pet and feed llamas and goats and see a variety of wild animals. Check the company website to find out about meet and greets with baby big cats and bears.
image source: Pixabay
Explore Bandon Beaches
Bandon has many beautiful beaches with unique rock formations and gorgeous views. This section of the coast is gorgeous in all weather and is perfect for having a beach day, watching the sunset, or just snapping a few selfies.
image source: Wikimedia commons
Horseback Beach Tours
Bandon Stables provides guided horseback tours for groups. Perfect for families!
image source: Pixabay
Face Rock Creamery
Check out locally-made cheese and ice cream, enjoy free samples, and be sure to pick up some of the famous "Vampire Killer" garlic cheese curds.
image source: Wikimedia commons
