Tour Newport

Aquarium of the Pacific
See sea otters eat and play, touch anemones in a tide pool, and watch sharks swim overhead in an underwater tunnel. Newport boasts the largest aquarium in Oregon, and has many exciting permanent features as well as visiting exhibitions.
image source: Pixabay
Yaquina Head Lighthouse
Call ahead of time to arrange a tour, or just enjoy the view of this cliff-top lighthouse, one of the oldest in Oregon.
image source: Pixabay
Yaquina Tidepools
The unique ecosystem of these pools shows visitors many unusual creatures that are usually hidden by the waves. In the Fall and Spring, seals may also be swimming nearby or napping on the rocks.
image source: Pixabay
Old Town
Explore a number of tasty seafood restaurants and souvenir shops. During migration season, you will see (and hear!) sea lions basking on the docks.
