Tour Astoria

Fort Clatsop
A replica of the encampment for Lewis and Clark during the the winter of 1805-1806. Learn about local history, stop for a picnic, and take a walk along one of the nearby forest trails.
image source: Wikipedia
Flavel House Museum
Historical Victorian house with period furnishings. Stop by for a tour and find out about life in Oregon during the Victorian period.
image source: Pixabay
Lightship Columbia
A historic lightship commissioned in 1951, this ship used to serve as a floating lighthouse for the Columbia River. Tour this ship as part of your admission to the Maritime Museum.
image source: Wikipedia
Astoria-Megler Bridge
The longest continous truss bridge in North America, this landmark serves as a great plce to enjoy the view or head over the Columbia River into Washington for a quick visit.
image source: Pixabay
